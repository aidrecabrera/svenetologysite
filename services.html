AIDRE: NEWEN CORPORATION

Read the business model

Newen Corporation is a publicly held corporation that designs and manufactures sustainable technologies and environmentally friendly products as well as LED lights and other extended services. Newen is founded by Aidre Cabrera, who wanted to promote and optimistic about the radical idea that sustainable technologies have a promising place in the market and industry and he wants to help accelerate the Philippines’ transition to sustainable transport, products, and energy. Newen’s main philosophy is that fossil fuels should be eliminated in the industry and people should stop hinging on fossil fuels and transition to a zero-emission future, thus, the purpose of this company is to make sustainable technologies available by filling in the gap in the market. Newen Technologies will be operated as a corporation and has a Chief Executive Officer as in the person of Aidre Love S. Cabrera: an individual who has no experience in anything whatsoever. As of now, he is independent but in the future if this company will be pursued, he will hire people and engineers to reach the main goal of this company as well as having a profit on the process.

Newen Corporation's Main Ideology
➔ We believe that Fossil fuel in the industry should be eliminated as it contributes to global warming, air pollution, environmental degradation, loss of coral reefs, wildfires, and etc.
➔ The world can attain a prosperous, healthy, and sustainable future if we act now. We believe that we must change the way we manufacture products and produce energy and we also believe that we must alter the conventional industry to a much eco-friendly and greener method in order to save humanity and solve the climate crisis.
➔ We also believe that the lack of initiative, transition plan, or strategy to sustainable energy sources and if we are unaware of the climate crisis, could lead to an economic and environmental downturn and could possibly lead to the downfall of humanity.

Newen Corporation's Main Vision
➔ We dream of a world with zero-emission, where sustainable and environmentally friendly products are utilized and fossil fuels aren't a necessity or an option anymore.
➔ We want to start a company that offers an innovative clean power source and sustainable and eco-friendly products.
➔ Non-Renewable resources in the industry are replaced with more Cheaper, Renewable, and Sustainable resources. Especially in the Philippines where I have observed that these types of products are overwhelmed by Non-renewables.

Newen Corporation's Main Goal
➔ I found that the Philippines lacks sustainable products or only a few are recognized. I want to fill in the gap in the market where sustainable and eco-friendly products are available.
➔ I want this company to be part of my advocacy and raise awareness of the Climate crisis and promote and persuade people to adapt to eco-friendly products. I also want to raise my ambition and commitment to help as much as I can to protect the environment.
